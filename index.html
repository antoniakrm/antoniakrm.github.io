About Me

I recently started my PhD at Copenhagen University joining the CoAStal NPL group under the supervision of Anders Søgaard. My main research interests are on Brain Activity-Language Model alignment, Explainable AI, Handwriting Recognition, Legal NLP, and Cultural NLP applications.

Master studies in Natural Language Processing from Uppsala University, provided me with the foundation for this research journey 🗺. My thesis was in Argument Mining and supervised by Joakim Nivre. I also have hands-on experience as a research assistant, data scientist and software engineer 💻.
